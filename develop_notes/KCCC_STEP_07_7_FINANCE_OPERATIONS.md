# KCCC Step 7.7 — Finance & Resources Operations

**Script ID:** `KCCC-STEP-07.7-FINANCE-OPERATIONS`  
**Status:** IN PROGRESS  
**Parent:** Step 7 Campaign Operations  

## Doctrine

> Finance & Resources Operations answers: **Do we have the resources to sustain the campaign?**

Not a ledger. Operational capacity / resource readiness only.

## Dual-state principle

> Every operational commitment has both an **operational state** and a **resource state**.

Operational state is consumed from Logistics. Resource state is owned here.

## Canonical ownership

Owns budget/resource readiness signals: finance-lead gaps, cost-bearing travel flags, fundraising calendar presence, dual-state mission rows, and feed Unknowns for dollars/approvals/cash.

Unknown: dollar amounts, cash position, pending approvals, purchase queue, reimbursements, vendors, county operating budgets.
