# KCCC Phase 2.4 — GOTV Operations

**Script ID:** `KCCC-PHASE-02.4-GOTV-OPERATIONS`  
**Status:** IN PROGRESS  
**Parent:** Phase 2 Capability Expansion  
**Route:** `/gotv`  
**Version target:** `0.8.3-gotv`

## Doctrine

> GOTV Operations answers: **Are we converting support into turnout?**

Coordinates execution across County, Volunteer, Constituent, Communications, Logistics, and Field — does not replicate those domains. Not a voter database.

## Owns (workflows)

- GOTV plans and phases  
- Turf execution workflows  
- Canvass and phone-bank campaigns  
- Chase programs  
- Early-vote operations  
- Election Day operations  
- Volunteer deployment plans  
- Poll-watching coordination  
- Daily turnout objectives  

## Does not own

- voter registration data  
- constituent relationship records  
- volunteer availability  
- county readiness  
- logistics facts  
- communications artifacts  

## Feeds

| Consumer | Consumes |
|----------|----------|
| Executive | GOTV Readiness, Today’s Deployment, Coverage Gaps, Priority Counties, Election Timeline, Turnout Risk |
| Candidate | GOTV-supporting stops, priority county visits, rally objectives, turnout messaging focus |
| County | turf coverage, deployment readiness, neighborhood execution, local election activities |
| Volunteer | canvass / phone-bank / poll-worker assignments, staging locations |
| Communications | GOTV messaging, election reminders, volunteer communications, rapid response needs |
| Intelligence | deployment trends, turnout risk, coverage imbalances, execution bottlenecks (interpret only) |

## Principle

> Capabilities coordinate execution across operational domains; they do not replicate those domains.
