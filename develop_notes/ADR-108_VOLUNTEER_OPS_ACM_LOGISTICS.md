# ADR-108 — Volunteer Ops, ACM, Campaign Logistics

```text
Accepted 2026-07-23 · IC-02D
```

## Decisions

1. **Assistant Campaign Manager** reports to Campaign Manager; coordinates campaign-wide work; cannot impersonate department approvals or open restricted finance detail.
2. **Campaign Logistics** reports directly to Campaign Manager (not under Volunteer or Ops&Data ownership); reuses D11–D13.
3. **Volunteer Manager home** is quiet by default: today, ≤5 deterministic priorities, urgent gaps, coordinator status, drill-downs.
4. **One work graph** via source-linked projections / lightweight index — not copied mutable truth.
5. **Volunteer identity ≠ consent ≠ assignment ≠ check-in ≠ Mission execution.**
6. **Deterministic priority** only — no OpenAI ranking in this pass.
