## Closing assessment

At the beginning of this effort, the project was primarily an ambitious collection of ideas.

At the close of Engineering Chapter 1, it has become a governed product with:

- a coherent architecture,  
- a clear operational philosophy,  
- disciplined implementation gates,

**Engineering Chapter 1 is complete.**
