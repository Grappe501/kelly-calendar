## Biggest insight

> **There is no persistent “What Matters Now?” layer.**

That is the single biggest UX gap.

Kelly should not wonder “Where do I click?” She should immediately know:

* what’s critical  
* what’s changed  
* what’s at risk  
* what’s next  

Everything else is secondary.
