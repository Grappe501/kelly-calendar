# KCCC Step 7.10 — Operational Intelligence

**Script ID:** `KCCC-STEP-07.10-OPERATIONAL-INTELLIGENCE`  
**Status:** IN PROGRESS  
**Parent:** Step 7 Campaign Operations  

## Doctrine

> Operational Intelligence answers: **What patterns, risks, and opportunities are emerging across the campaign?**

## Non-override principle

> Operational Intelligence may interpret canonical facts, but it never replaces or overrides them.

Owns: cross-domain interpretation, risk prioritization, deterministic forecasts from feeds, executive insight packing.

Does **not** own: Field help queue, county health, volunteer capacity, communications plans, logistics readiness, finance resource state, compliance status.
