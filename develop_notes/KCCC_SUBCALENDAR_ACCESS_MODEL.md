# KCCC Subcalendar Access Model

Teams own calendars; other teams see **limited occupied blocks** so Kelly’s day remains understandable.

## Examples

### Communications

- Full/access: Communications, Press and Media, Social Media, approved public events
- Limited blocks: Travel, Fundraising, Personal time, Internal meetings

### Fundraising

- Full/access: Fundraising, finance calls, donor events
- Limited blocks: Travel, Public events, Communications, Personal time

### County organizer

- Full/access: County, local volunteer, local public events, assigned visits
- Limited blocks: other calendars when needed for availability

### Social media

- Full/access: Social Media, content production, recording, public events
- Limited context: surrounding candidate day without protected details

## Permission vocabulary

`NO_ACCESS` · `AVAILABILITY_ONLY` · `VIEW` · `CONTRIBUTE` · `EDIT` · `MANAGE` · `ADMINISTER`

**AVAILABILITY_ONLY** (amended doctrine): still shows primary calendar name, safe title, general location when safe, and times — not protected operational fields.

Primary calendar label is used for multi-calendar events; additional memberships are not exposed by default.
