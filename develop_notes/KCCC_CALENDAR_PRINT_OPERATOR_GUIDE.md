# Calendar Print — Operator Guide (CC-12)

## Open print preview

1. From Calendar, use the **Print** chip on the view switcher, or open `/system/calendar/print/preview`.
2. Choose day / week / agenda and a privacy profile.
3. Open the sheet, then use **Print** (browser print dialog).

Health and subscriptions headers also offer a small **Print** chip for convenience.

## Profiles

| Profile | Use when |
|---------|----------|
| Day operations (redacted) | Field day sheets — city only |
| Internal day detail | Elevated ops — venue + city, still no street |
| Week overview | Weekly planning — titles + city + status |

## Safety

- Sheets exclude street addresses, private notes, feed tokens, and contacts by policy.
- Do not paste internal sheets into public channels.
- Print does **not** change Events, Missions, or ICS feeds.

## Mobile

On a phone, prefer **Agenda list view** from Day/Week toolbars if the grid is too dense, then print agenda if needed.
