# ADR-107 — Four-department operating structure

```text
Accepted 2026-07-23 · IC-02C
```

## Decision

Steve’s four direct operating reports are:

1. Volunteer and Organizing Manager  
2. Communications Manager  
3. Finance Manager  
4. Operations and Data Coordinator  

Operations and Data is the connective tissue (calendar, activation routing, logistics, systems) — not a third split of Scheduling/Logistics/Data yet.

Positions are seats, not people. Access derives from ACTIVE/INTERIM assignments and reporting relationships — not title strings. One canonical Event/Mission/task set; department calendars are derived views.

Six draft clusters + 75 vacant County Captains (IC-01) with no invented membership or fabricated people.
