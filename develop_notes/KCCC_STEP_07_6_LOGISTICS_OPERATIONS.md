## Doctrine

> Logistics Operations answers: **Can we actually execute today’s plan?**

## Minimum readiness principle

> Operational readiness equals the minimum readiness of all required operational domains.

One BLOCKED domain ⇒ mission BLOCKED. Never “75% ready.”

## Canonical ownership

Owns travel plan readiness, driver-assigned gaps, packing fill, venue completeness, lodging requirement flags.

Unknown: vehicle fleet, live ETA, booking confirmations, equipment registry, county inventory.
